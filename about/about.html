About Me

Hey there. I'm Jepchirchir Mercy, but you can call me Mercy Melly—or MM if you're feeling fancy.I love a good conversation, so whether it's a simple hello or a deep dive into random topics, I'm always up for it.

I am a creative soul with a love for all things hands-on and expressive. When I'm not lost in my latest crochet project, you'll probably find me dreaming up new hobbies to dive into—because why settle for just one?

Right now, I'm on a mission to explore cadistry (because who doesn't want to master the art of card flourishing?), play the piano (slowly but surely), and learn multiple languages. Life is too short to stick to just one thing, so I'm embracing all the little passions that make me, well… me. Whether it's crafting, learning, or just figuring things out as I go, I'm all about enjoying the journey.
